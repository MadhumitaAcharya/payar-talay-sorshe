Privacy Policy

StayHub collects personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and payment details to facilitate reservations and provide personalized services.

Personal information provided by guests is used solely for the purpose of managing reservations, processing payments, and enhancing the overall guest experience. StayHub does not sell or share guest information with third parties for marketing purposes.

StayHub implements industry-standard security measures to protect guest information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Cookie Policy

StayHub uses cookies to enhance the browsing experience and track website usage. Cookies may be used for analytics, advertising, and improving website functionality. Guests have the option to disable cookies in their browser settings, although this may affect certain features of the website.

Third-Party Links

The StayHub website may contain links to third-party websites. StayHub is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. Guests are encouraged to review the privacy policies of third-party sites before providing any personal information.

Policy Updates

StayHub reserves the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes to the policy will be communicated through the website or other appropriate channels.
